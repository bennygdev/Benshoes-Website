How the company started

The company basically started as a small group of friends promoting shoes and selling used shoes to family members and friends, and eventually this turned out to be a dream hobby of us to sell shoes on the market. We personally enjoyed selling shoes and we decide to make this a reality by providing shoes for the community!

What we do now

We now sell shoes for a living now! Thanks to everyone who supported this and made this a reality. We sell shoes ranging from school to sports shoes, we are trying to expand our curated recommendations of shoes and try to cater it to everyone
